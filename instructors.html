Anna Landauer

Mindful Vinyasa Flow
  • Tuesdays Level 1/2
  • 12:00pm - 1:00pm
  • Thursdays Level 2/3
  • 10:00am - 11:15am

Anna Landauer

Anna Landauer has practiced yoga since youth and began teaching in 1998. She trained in classical Hatha Yoga at the Ashtanga Yoga Institute, and in Anusara Yoga at Seattle Yoga Arts. She integrates the strong alignment traditions of BKS Iyengar, the graceful heart opening attitude of Anusara yoga with the flowing sequences of Vinyasa yoga in her teaching.

Mindful Vinyasa Flow

This energizing and vigorous style of yoga consists of a continuous series of flowing postures linked together with breath. This class integrates meditation and mindfulness practice with classical yoga asana, encouraging heightened awareness of breath and body in order to guide one’s attention to the present moment. An emphasis is placed on self knowledge and self care, as students are guided to listen and respond skillfully to the wisdom of their own body. Mindful vinyasa blends the strong alignment traditions of BKS Iyengar (aka Hatha yoga), and attention to the present moment, with a graceful heart opening attitude and the flowing sequences of Vinyasa yoga.

Johanna Lelke

Hatha Flow Yoga
  • Wednesdays Level 1/2
  • 12:00pm - 1:00pm

Johanna Lelke

Johanna has been a yoga teacher since 2006 while in chiropractic school in Portland, OR. She transplanted to the East Bay in 2009 and resumed teaching yoga within her beloved rock climbing community. Johanna has a deep appreciation for Iyengar alignment, yin restorative principles, and body rolling—-blending these qualities into her flow classes. As a sports chiropractor, she’s able to offer a keen eye for correcting unhealthy alignment among her yoga students.

Hatha Flow Yoga

In this beginner to intermediate class, explore the foundational postures and practice merging the breath with movement. Students of all experience levels are welcome to join, as we slow down the transitions in order to become more body aware. There is some hands-on assisting in order to help each student cultivate their most healthy and proper alignment. Johanna teaches from the perspective of a chiropractor yogi, and fundamentally honors the heart connection between each of us.
