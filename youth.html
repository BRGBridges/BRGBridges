Team Bridges Rock Gym (Ages 6-19)

Team Bridges Rock Gym (Ages 6-19)

For dedicated climbers looking to compete both locally and nationally, Team Bridges offers the opportunity to train with our coaches in a serious environment with other young athletes. Team Bridges competes in Northern California Youth Climbing League and USA Climbing American Bouldering Series Competitions, and will push climbers' limits through conditioning, strength training, and mental focus.

  • Must have prior climbing experience or receive recommendation from a coach.
  • Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays
  • 4:00pm - 6:00pm
  • $125/mo
  • Includes unlimited benefits of membership and gear rental
Teen Climbing (Ages 10-18)

Teen Climbing (Ages 10-18)

Our Teen Climbing Program is dedicated to working with all levels of teen climbers that would like to take their climbing to a higher level. We provide a fun and supportive environment for our teen climbers to work on specific skills with organized climbing goals.

  • Tuesdays & Thursdays
  • 4:00pm - 6:00pm
  • $90/mo
  • Includes unlimited benefits of membership and gear rental
After School Climbing (Ages 5-12)

After School Climbing (Ages 5-12)

Our After School Climbing program is an excellent way to help kids stay active and in shape! Kids will learn climbing technique and safety by bouldering and top-rope climbing. Training in the slackline arena is a unique and exciting way for kids to improve their balance and skill in any sport!

  • Mondays & Wednesdays
  • 4:00pm - 6:00pm
  • $140/mo
  • Includes unlimited benefits of membership and gear rental
Kid's Camp

Kid's Camp

Looking for something fun for the kids over the upcoming break? Our kids' camps are action-packed, educational, and super fun! Week-long camp sessions are held Monday through Friday. Participants will receive expert instruction in climbing and slacklining, learn about safety, play fun games, and acquire some fantastic skills in the process.

  • First Spring Camp 04/03 - 04/07
  • Second Spring Camp 04/10 - 04/14
  • Summer Camp Starts 06/12
  • Enrollment for ALL camps OPEN. Call to sign up!

  • Hours
  • Full Day 9:00am to 3:00pm
  • Half Day 9:00am to 12:00pm or 12:00pm to 3:00pm

  • Pricing per Week:

  • Half Day: $249 ($10.96/hr)
  • Members receive a $30 discount!

  • Pricing for Drop-In:
